Streamer Rules

1. Respect Your Audience

Always treat your viewers with respect. Toxicity or harassment in your streams will not be tolerated.

2. Be Transparent

Honesty and transparency with your audience is key to building trust. Be open about sponsorships, advertisements, and giveaways.

3. No Hate Speech

Racist, sexist, homophobic, or discriminatory language is strictly prohibited. Create an inclusive and welcoming environment.

4. Keep Content PG

Ensure your content is suitable for all audiences. Explicit or inappropriate content can lead to platform bans.

5. LAD (LIVE ADMIN DISUCCSION)

Sream must be muted when your are talking to a admin in the server! (In support section)

6. Respect Copyright

Make sure your content adheres to copyright laws. Do not stream or share copyrighted music or media without proper rights.

7. Protect Privacy

Do not share personal or private information, either your own or others. Be mindful of your privacy and the privacy of your community.

8. Be Mindful of Your Moderators

Moderators are essential for keeping the community safe. Listen to them, follow their instructions, and respect their decisions.

9. Avoid Self-Promotion

Do not excessively promote your own or others' channels, products, or services in the chat. Let the content speak for itself.

10. Have Fun!

Streaming should be fun! Keep a positive attitude and make sure your audience has an enjoyable time watching.

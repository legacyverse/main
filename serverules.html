Server Rules

1. No Cheating or Hacking

While Anarchy servers are lawless, cheating using mods, hacks, or external tools to gain an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited. Play the game with your skills!

2. No Exploiting Bugs

Abusing game bugs to exploit the server or gain benefits is not allowed. Report any bugs immediately to admins for fair play.

3. Dont Abuse

Abusing anyone in private/discord chat or in Ingame chat leades to punishment

4. Respect Others' Builds (Only if You’re Not Griefing)

Griefing and destruction are allowed in the purest Anarchy style, but do not excessively ruin others' creations for the sake of ruining the game. Keep chaos fun!

5. No Advertising

Avoid advertising other servers, websites, or irrelevant external content. This keeps the server focused on the Anarchy experience.

6. No Duplication or Exploitative Glitches

Using glitches or exploits to duplicate items will result in punishment. Only use what the game gives you!

7. Play Fair – No "Ghosting" or "Alt Accounts"

Ghosting or using multiple accounts to gain an advantage over others is considered cheating. Stick to your own account.

8. No Spamming

Spamming chat with repetitive messages, commands, or irrelevant text is not allowed. Keep the chat readable.

9. Use Common Sense

Even in anarchy, keep some level of decency. This includes not harassing other players outside of the game or spreading personal information.

10. No AFK Farming

Automated or AFK farming (such as using bots or scripts for farming or gaining items) will lead to a ban. Keep the play active and challenging!

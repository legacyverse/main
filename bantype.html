Ban Types

1. Temporary Ban

This ban will last from 2-3 days even up to a month. The player will be temporarily restricted from participating in the server, depending on the severity of the offense.

2. Season Ban

This ban will last for the current Legacy Verse Minecraft season. Once the season ends, the player will be allowed to rejoin the server and continue their gameplay.

3. Permanent Ban

This ban will last as long as Legacy Verse exists. A permanent ban means that the player will never be able to access the server again unless the ban is lifted through a special request.
